Selected Projects (Section in construction)

Hand augmentation   Data Preprocessing  Statistical Analysis

In the recent years there has been an increased interest in augmentative technologies aimed to extend the physical and cognitive abilities of our bodies. But while tremendous resources are being dedicated to the development of augmentative devices, little notice is given to how the human brain might support them. This repository contains the codebase of a research project, exploring the brain changes following hand augmentation. The outcomes of this research have been published in Science Robotics.

Targeting customers - Uplift   Machine Learning

How do the marketing campaigns identify the right individuals to target? Here, I used historical Starbucks data to identify the customers, who are likely to purchase the product only if offered a promotion. I used both traditional propensity models and causal uplift modelling, increasing the incremental response rate of the campaign 2.5 times.

Automated PDF data extraction   Data Extraction

Many companies have valuable information stored in huge amounts of analog or digital documents (e.g. pdf files). Their volume makes manual data extraction unfeasible. Here, I implemented a system for automatic information extraction, organising data from html and pdf files. This system was used at the hospital in Łęczyca (Poland) to catalogue the amount of contracted and realized medical services.

Find out more

Visualising dipolar brain sources   Data Visualisation

Many independent EEG components have maps that nearly perfectly match the projection of a single equivalent dipole. Here, I explored ways of visualising those equivalent dipoles in 3D, taking into account the variability originating from the ICA decomposition. I used bootstrapped ICA to obtain several estimations of each dipole's position and calculated equivalent 3D ellipsoids. I also projected the estimated dipoles onto the cortical surface.
